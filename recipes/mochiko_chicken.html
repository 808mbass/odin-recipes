Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup mochiko flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1 stalk green onion, chopped fine
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions:

  1. Skin and de-bone chicken thighs, set aside.
  2. Combine remaining ingredients in large zip-lock bag or container, add chicken and marinate overnight.
  3. Pan fry chicken thighs until golden brown and crispy on outside and until chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees farenheit.
  4. Garnish with fresh green onions or serve plain with white rice and Hawaiian style macaroni salad.
